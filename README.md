## About

---

* FrontEngine can draw below type on front of your screen.
* You can use your computer as usual without being disturbed.
* FrontEngine can play multi instance.
  * Video.
  * Website.
  * GIF & WEBP.
  * Image.
  * Play sound.
  * Text.
* And FrontEngine is super easy to use.
